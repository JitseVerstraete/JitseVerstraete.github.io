About Me

Hey! I'm game development student at Digital Arts and Entertainment in Kortrijk. My passion lies in creating games, with a particular focus on gameplay and AI programming. I'm currently looking for an end of study internship starting in September 2023!

I've always had a fascination with video games, which is in part where my interest in game development originates from. Before i started my studies at DAE, I studied Applied Informatics for one semester, where i got my first taste of programming. I decided to combine my love for video games and my new found interest in programming and enroll in Digital Arts & Entertainment.

When I'm not hard at work, I enjoy playing music to help me relax and unwind. I learned to play the drums when i was in music school and i taught myself how to play guitar. Besides music i also love playing video games. Some of my favorite games include Satisfactory, Sea of Thieves, Outer Wilds, Risk of Rain 2, Dead Cells... I would love to be part of a team that make awesome games like these.

If you have any questions, don't hesitate to contact me through my socials, email or by calling me!
