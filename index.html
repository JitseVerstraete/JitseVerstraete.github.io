About Me

I'm a game development graduate from Digital Arts and Entertainment in Kortrijk. During my studies, I have developed both my skills and my passion for making creative gaming experiences.

Prior to embarking on my studies at DAE, I studied Applied Informatics for one semester, where I discovered my interest in programming. Combined with my enthusiasm for video games, I decided to learn the skills needed to create games and enrolled in Howest's Game development curriculum.

In my free time, I enjoy playing music to help me relax and unwind. I learned to play the drums when I was in music school and I taught myself how to play guitar. Besides music, I also love playing video games. Some of my favorite games include Satisfactory, Sea of Thieves, Outer Wilds, Risk of Rain 2 and Dead Cells. I would love to be part of a team that makes awesome games like these.

If you have any questions, don't hesitate to contact me through my socials, email or by calling me!
