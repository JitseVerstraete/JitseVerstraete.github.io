Tripsitter

Small game created during the Global game jam 2024

Game jam page Download Source code

Technologies

Unity, C#

Diciplines

Gameplay programming, Game Design

Duration

48 Hour game jam

Project Description

Your friend, who has taken a few too many psychedelics, calls you in a panic and asks you for your help. It is your job as the player to make sure they don't go into a "bad trip". You do this by making sure they are comfortable and by eliminating any disturbing stimuli. Giving them food when they're hungry, putting the TV on an enjoyable channel or opening/closing the window blinds are the actions you can perform to make sure they have a good trip.
